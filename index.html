SpongeBob

SpongeBob SquarePants is an American animated television series created by marine biologist Stephen Hillenburg . The series began production in 1999 by Nickelodeon Animation Studios .

The setting of SpongeBob SquarePants is Bikini Bottom, an underwater city in the Pacific Ocean. SpongeBob SquarePants was born on July 14th, 1986 according to his driver's license. Despite his name, Squidward is an octopus. He only has six tentacles though, not the usual eight that an octopus would have.
